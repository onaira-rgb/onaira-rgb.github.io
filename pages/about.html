Our Story

About OUBA

A women-owned engineering firm built on international expertise and a commitment to communities in motion.

Mission

Engineering solutions that move communities forward

OUBA Engineers and Consultants LLC is a Boston-based, women-owned multidisciplinary engineering and consulting firm. Founded by Onaira Zahoor, OUBA brings together expertise in transportation planning, environmental engineering, civil infrastructure, and data analytics to serve public agencies and private clients across the United States.

We believe in combining rigorous technical analysis with a genuine understanding of community needs — delivering solutions that are practical, sustainable, and grounded in real-world evidence.

Credentials

Business Certifications

OUBA is pursuing and eligible for key small business certifications that open doors to public sector contracting.

WOSB

Women-Owned Small Business

OUBA is majority-owned and operated by a woman, qualifying for WOSB set-aside contracts at the federal level.

MBE

Minority Business Enterprise

Eligible for MBE/DBE certification, unlocking access to state and local set-aside programs, including MassDOT DBE goals.

SB

Small Business

Registered small business eligible for SBA set-aside contracts across federal agencies including DOT, FTA, and FHWA.
