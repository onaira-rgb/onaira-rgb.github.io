Our People

The OUBA Team

Four engineers with complementary disciplines and a shared commitment to rigorous, client-focused work.

OZ

Onaira Zahoor

Founder & Principal Engineer
Environmental Engineering Transportation Railway Infrastructure

Onaira is a results-driven environmental and transportation engineer with over 12 years of professional experience spanning wastewater treatment, environmental impact assessment, railway infrastructure, and transportation systems. She founded OUBA to bring her multidisciplinary expertise to US clients and public agencies.

Her graduate research at Southeast University focused on traffic safety analysis and transportation data techniques, earning top marks in Road Traffic Safety Theory and Advanced Road Design. At Pakistan Railways, she led large-scale infrastructure planning and rehabilitation projects, integrating environmental protection throughout project lifecycles.

Education
  • M.Eng. Communication & Transportation Engineering — Southeast University, China (2019)
  • M.Sc. Environmental Engineering — NUST, Pakistan (2013)
  • B.Sc. Civil Engineering — UET Peshawar (2011)
MU

Muhammad Usama

Transportation Data Specialist
Transit Planning Data Analytics GIS GTFS / AVL / APC

Muhammad specializes in transportation data science, transit planning, and mobility analytics. He brings expertise in processing and analyzing GTFS, AVL, and APC datasets; building performance dashboards; and applying GIS tools for equity analysis and corridor studies.

He has experience supporting federal grant applications for FTA and FHWA programs, and has developed data pipelines and visualization tools for transit agencies. His background spans public transit planning, multimodal analysis, and origin-destination studies using third-party mobility data platforms.

Key Expertise
  • Transit data analytics: GTFS, AVL, APC data processing
  • GIS mapping and geospatial analysis (ArcGIS, QGIS)
  • Mobility analytics: StreetLight, Replica, APC
  • Federal grant writing: FTA, FHWA, RAISE programs
AH

Asad Hassan

Senior Civil & Infrastructure Engineer
Civil Infrastructure PM Road Design Directional Drilling Gyroscopic Survey

Asad is a senior engineering professional with over 12 years of progressive experience across oil and gas directional drilling, subsurface survey operations, and civil infrastructure project management. He has led complex multi-site infrastructure projects in 11 countries across the Middle East, Central Asia, and South Asia.

As a Directional Driller at SLB (Schlumberger), he manages well trajectory control, BHA design, and real-time drilling optimization for internationally recognized clients including ADNOC and Qatar Petroleum. His civil infrastructure experience includes road rehabilitation, sewerage, drainage, and pavement design for urban projects in Pakistan.

Education
  • B.Sc. Civil Engineering — University of Engineering & Technology (UET), Lahore (2013)
AB

Abu Bakar

Quantity Surveyor & Project Manager
Quantity Take-Off Cost Estimation Project Management Site Engineering

Abu Bakar is a civil engineer with 12 years of specialized expertise in quantity surveying, cost estimation, and construction project management. He has a strong track record of delivering accurate quantity take-offs and cost estimates for road, utility, and building projects from concept through completion.

His project management experience includes site supervision, procurement coordination, and compliance reporting for civil infrastructure works. He is a key asset for clients requiring reliable cost control and procurement support at any stage of a project.

Key Expertise
  • Detailed quantity take-off and measurement
  • Construction cost estimating and budget control
  • Site engineering and quality inspection
  • Procurement documentation and contractor coordination
