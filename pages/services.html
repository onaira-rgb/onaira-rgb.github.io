What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive engineering and consulting solutions — from transportation planning to environmental compliance and infrastructure delivery.

01
Transportation Engineering

Transportation Planning & Engineering

We provide end-to-end transportation planning and engineering services for public agencies, developers, and municipalities. Our team brings deep knowledge of US and international transport systems to deliver actionable, compliant solutions.

Traffic Impact Studies (TIS)

Trip generation, distribution, and level-of-service analysis for development projects and permitting.

Transportation Demand Management (TDM)

Mode split analysis, TDM plans, and monitoring frameworks required by municipalities for large developments.

Multimodal Corridor Studies

Assessment of pedestrian, bicycle, transit, and vehicle movements along urban and suburban corridors.

Traffic Safety Analysis

Crash data analysis, high-risk segment identification, and countermeasure recommendations using statistical methods.

Parking Studies

Parking demand analysis using ITE rates, local data, and shared/dynamic parking modeling for developers and institutions.

Railway Infrastructure Consulting

Planning, design review, and project management for rail corridor upgrades and station facility improvements.

02
Data & Analytics

Transit Data Analytics & Mobility Intelligence

Leveraging GTFS, AVL, APC, and third-party mobility data, we deliver rigorous data science solutions that help agencies and operators understand their systems and make evidence-based decisions.

GTFS / AVL / APC Processing

Data pipeline development, cleaning, and analysis for transit schedule, vehicle, and ridership datasets.

Transit Performance Dashboards

Interactive dashboards for on-time performance, headway adherence, and ridership trends using Tableau or Power BI.

Origin-Destination Analysis

Mobility pattern studies using StreetLight, Replica, and APC data to understand travel behavior.

GIS & Geospatial Analysis

ArcGIS and QGIS mapping, equity analysis, crash hotspot mapping, and accessibility modeling.

Data Mining for Transportation

Statistical and machine learning methods applied to transportation datasets for planning and safety insights.

Transit Service Planning

Ridership analysis, route optimization, frequency studies, and service change impact assessments.

03
Environmental Engineering

Environmental Engineering & Compliance

Our environmental engineers have extensive experience with impact assessments, wastewater systems, and regulatory compliance. We help clients navigate complex environmental requirements efficiently.

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA/IEE)

Baseline data collection, impact prediction, and mitigation planning for infrastructure and development projects.

Wastewater Treatment Design

Design and evaluation of treatment systems including Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) technology; effluent quality analysis (BOD, COD, TSS).

Air & Noise Pollution Assessment

Monitoring, modeling, and mitigation planning for air quality and noise impacts from transportation and industrial projects.

Solid Waste Management

Waste stream analysis, management planning, and regulatory compliance support.

Environmental Management Plans

Project-level EMP development and regulatory agency coordination for environmental clearance.

Water Resource Management

Stormwater planning, drainage design, and water quality assessments for infrastructure projects.

04
Civil Engineering

Civil Infrastructure & Project Management

From road rehabilitation to underground utilities, our civil team delivers complex infrastructure projects on time and within budget. We bring international experience in managing multi-site, multi-disciplinary projects.

Road Design & Rehabilitation

Pavement design, road reconstruction, geometric improvements, and drainage design for urban and rural roadways.

Sewerage & Drainage Systems

Design, construction management, and inspection of sewerage and stormwater drainage infrastructure.

Construction Project Management

Critical path scheduling, cost control, SIMOPS coordination, and contractor management for civil works.

Geotechnical Assessment

Supervision of geotechnical investigations, soil analysis, and foundation recommendations for infrastructure projects.

Site Engineering & Inspection

Site supervision, inspection test plans (ITPs), material testing, and as-built documentation.

Subsurface & Utility Engineering

Underground utility conflict resolution, subsurface survey, and pipeline route assessment support.

05
Cost & Procurement

Cost Estimation & Quantity Take-Off

Accurate cost planning is critical to project success. Our quantity surveyors and civil engineers deliver detailed, reliable estimates from concept through construction.

Detailed Quantity Take-Off (QTO)

Comprehensive material and work quantity calculations for roads, utilities, buildings, and civil structures.

Construction Cost Estimating

Conceptual, schematic, and detailed estimates aligned with project phases and procurement strategy.

Budget Forecasting & Control

Cash flow projections, budget tracking, and variance analysis throughout project execution.

Procurement Support

Bid document preparation, scope of work writing, and contractor bid evaluation support.

06
Federal Funding

Federal Grant Writing & Program Support

We help public agencies, non-profits, and transit operators navigate federal funding programs — from crafting competitive grant applications to managing reporting requirements.

FTA Grant Applications

5307, 5310, 5311, and Capital Investment Grants (CIG) — application writing, cost-effectiveness analysis, and submittal support.

FHWA / RAISE Grant Support

Competitive federal highway and discretionary grant applications with narrative writing and data support.

Program Management Oversight (PMO)

Federal grant compliance, progress reporting, and documentation management for active grant programs.

Technical Writing & Documentation

Reports, white papers, and technical documentation for transportation planning and policy projects.
